    Did you know?

    Globally there are more than 1 billion people that are visually impaired, affecting their ability to perform every tasks such as chores andself-care

    How is it to be visually impaired??

    Imagine having no way of correcting your vision without using every day equipment like glasses or contact lenses. You are therefore challenged by these very common every day tasks:


  • Defning whether am object is moving or not.
  • Able to catch up or save time by running ourside.
  • Always have to look out for object you may bump yourself into.

    • What technology can you rely on?

    In your disposal you have plenty but not very efficient ways of helping yourself out.

  • An app that requires you to look down at every so often
  • A separate pair of hearing devices that tells you about your surroundings.
  • *A feedback device that nudges you and distracts you from your other two devices.

    • Thats only the first part
