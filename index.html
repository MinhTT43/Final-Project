    How is it to be visually impaired??

    Imagine having no way of correcting your vision without using every day equipment like glasses or contact lenses. You are therefore challenged by these very common every day tasks:


  • Defning whether am object is moving or not.
  • Able to catch up or save time by running ourside.
  • Always have to look out for object you may bump yourself into.
