    • Joint review of the assignment and the sustainable development goals.
    • Each group of the groupe choose and shared some of their favorite ideas from the innovation method assignment. And discussed possible issues related to the ideas


    Some of the ideas that was presented:

    - Put effort in to making insects a more realistic food source by producing products that we are familiar with (burgers, hot-dogs, soups...) to reduce the pollution the meat industry has on the environment.

    - Paying people to try insect food and show that it can be just as good. This will replace some fish and meat that will lead to less Co2 and acidification of the ocean.

    - A travel app, that recommends travel locations that is located in the least developed countries. This will increase their tourism revenue and further develop the country, and thus make it easier for said countries to adapt sustainable use of marine resources.

    - Create new standard for modular home solar power systems, greatly reducing cost of equipment and installation.

    - Implementing a gardening club/class to schools of any level to encourage people to learn how to grow their own vegetables and such to promote healthy living and the importance of proper food-

    - A clock based on the pomodoro technique that includes a small section for workouts/other activities, for once the timer has run out. To keep you productive AND healthy.

    - A club for older people that will introduce new experiences/hobbies for them where they can meet new people, socialize and get to do something out of the ordinary.

    - In the same way oil contracts are given to other nations, fishing contracts can be given to less developed countries and the fish sold to the more developed countries for the local price. This way the wealth can be distributed more evenly among the countries.

    - A product/policy that would give the end user easy access to information where the product is produced and how much the workers were paid, in an attempt to play on the end users empathy. This might give the end users more power to choose and force the employers in the production end to raise salaries and eventually raise the less developed countries ability to apply sustainability for the environment.
